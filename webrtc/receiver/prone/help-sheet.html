Prone detection – Help

Goal. Detect sustained face-down (prone) posture by combining face visibility and head pitch from MediaPipe Face Landmarker.

Calibrate supine. With the baby face-up, press Start for a few seconds. The average pitch is saved as the baseline.

Risk bar. Shows risk as a percentage of the app’s max occlusion-only risk (0.6). Colors: green (low), orange (elevated), red (alert).

Hysteresis. Alert latches after risk > thr on for dwell on seconds; clears after risk < thr off for dwell off seconds.

This tool is not a medical device. Use as an assistive cue only.
